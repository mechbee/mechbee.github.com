About Us

We are two developers/entrepreneurs working out of Manhattan with experience in the entire spectrum of web development. We love working on and with startups. Have a startup idea? We can make your MVP. for more info.

Our own projects include:

Givmo

Givmo is an online marketplace for people to give away their stuff and get other stuff for just the cost of shipping.

Rails, CouchDB, Amazon Payments, Twitter and Facebook sharing

Where To Eat At

Where To Eat At is a solution to the question of where to eat lunch. We make deciding easier by remembering your preferences to give you just 3 good choices a day.

Rails, CouchDB, Yelp API
